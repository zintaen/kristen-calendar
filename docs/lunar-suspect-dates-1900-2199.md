# Lunar suspect-dates report (1900-2199)

Generated by diffing the shipping lunar engine (@cyberskill/amlich-core) against the astronomy-engine ephemeris. Two lists:

1. Ephemeris mismatches: month-starts where the Ho Ngoc Duc method assigns a different civil day than a full ephemeris. These are the dates where the app's lunar date could be off by the listed offset. Per decision 3 (DEC-LUNAR-039) these are flag-and-accept inside 1900-2100 and within-1-day-accept for 2100-2199; spot-check each against the official Ho Ngoc Duc calculator (https://www.informatik.uni-leipzig.de/~duc/amlich/) before relying on it.
2. Fragile boundaries: month-starts that currently agree with the ephemeris but where the true new moon falls within 15 minutes of VN midnight, so a small future refinement could flip them. Informational.

Method note: civil day = floor(newMoon_JD + 0.5 + 7/24), the exact rule the engine uses. Times are VN local (UTC+7, 105E). This is a dev-time cross-check; the core stays zero-dependency and offline.
